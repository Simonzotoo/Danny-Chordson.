About Me

A dedicated professional with a unique blend of expertise in business and the creative arts. I hold a first degree in Marketing from UPSA and a second degree in Procurement from UCC, providing a strong foundation in strategic sourcing, supply chain management, and brand development.

Parallel to my academic journey, I have cultivated a 20-year career as a versatile musician. Beginning with drums, I expanded my repertoire to master the piano and learnt the bass guitar.

I am skilled at bridging the gap between creative vision and operational execution, making me a valuable asset in any project-driven environment.
